Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Local-first by design

Formo does not require an account. Your profile, measurements, meals, workouts and progress records are designed to stay on your device.

Camera and photos

Formo asks for camera or photo-library access only when you choose a feature that needs it, such as barcode scanning, meal photos or private progress photos.

Optional AI features

If you choose AI meal photo, the selected image is sent through our Supabase function to OpenAI for analysis. It is not added to your progress gallery or written to Formo tables, and Formo does not send photos in the background. OpenAI API abuse-monitoring logs may retain customer content for up to 30 days by default unless the production project is approved and configured for a reduced-retention control.

Premium purchases

RevenueCat and your app store process Premium purchases. Formo uses an anonymous device-linked customer identifier to verify your entitlement; we do not ask for your name, email address or a Formo account.

Online service data

The AI service records the anonymous customer identifier, request date and daily request count to enforce fair-use limits. Meal photos are not written to the Formo database by the analysis function.

Your control

You can choose which permissions to grant and can change them in your device settings. Removing the app removes locally stored Formo data from the device. App-store purchase records remain subject to the store and RevenueCat retention rules.
